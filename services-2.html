Custom Packages




Your domain name is the ".com" or ".co.za" part of your website address.
This can even be a custom name such as .io or .ly. You can be creative and integrate your business or blog name into this.
For example a delivery company could use the domain name box making their web address www.delivery.box


Your website files and content needs to be saved on a physical drive. Where this is stored can vary, however using professional hosting services ensures the security of your data.



How many pages do you need?

It depends on what you need your website to do. If you only need to promote your app, you might need just 1 page.

If you simply want online booking and information you could use 3. A store front or interactive site would need between 4 and 10 pages.
